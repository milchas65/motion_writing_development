# Summary Judgment Reply Overview

## Purpose
Rebut opponent's arguments, address new evidence, and reinforce entitlement to summary judgment.

## Key Principles

### 1. Don't Repeatâ€”Rebut
Respond to opponent's specific arguments, not your opening brief.

### 2. Highlight Failures
Show what opponent failed to dispute or adequately address.

### 3. Address New Evidence
Respond to affidavits or evidence submitted with opposition.

### 4. Maintain Focus
Address only arguments opponent actually made.

## Brief Structure

```
REPLY MEMORANDUM OF LAW

[Preamble]

PRELIMINARY STATEMENT
[Frame opponent's concessions and failures]

ARGUMENT

I. PLAINTIFF'S PURPORTED DISPUTES ARE NOT GENUINE
   A. [Address first claimed dispute]
   B. [Address second claimed dispute]

II. PLAINTIFF'S LEGAL ARGUMENTS FAIL
   [Rebut legal contentions]

CONCLUSION
```

## Tone
- Confident but professional
- Focus on record evidence
- Not dismissive or hostile
