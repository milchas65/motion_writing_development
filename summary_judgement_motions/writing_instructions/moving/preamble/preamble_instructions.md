# Preamble Instructions (Summary Judgment Motion - Moving)

## Purpose
The preamble identifies the document and states the relief sought.

## Format

```
MEMORANDUM OF LAW IN SUPPORT OF
[DEFENDANT'S/PLAINTIFF'S] MOTION FOR SUMMARY JUDGMENT

[Party] respectfully submits this memorandum of law in support of its motion for summary judgment [dismissing the complaint / on its counterclaims / etc.].
```

## Variations

### Standard Motion
> Defendant ABC Corp. respectfully submits this memorandum of law in support of its motion for summary judgment dismissing the complaint in its entirety.

### Cross-Motion + Opposition
> Defendants respectfully submit this memorandum of law in support of their cross-motion for summary judgment dismissing the complaint and in opposition to Plaintiff's motion for summary judgment.

### Partial Summary Judgment
> Plaintiff respectfully submits this memorandum of law in support of its motion for partial summary judgment on its breach of contract claim.
