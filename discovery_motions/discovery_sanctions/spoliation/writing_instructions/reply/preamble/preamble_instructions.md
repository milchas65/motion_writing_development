# Preamble Instructions (Spoliation Sanctions Reply)

## Purpose
Identify this document as a reply memorandum in further support of the spoliation motion.

## Format

```
REPLY MEMORANDUM OF LAW IN FURTHER SUPPORT OF
MOTION FOR SPOLIATION SANCTIONS

Plaintiff [NAME] respectfully submits this reply memorandum of law in further support of its motion for spoliation sanctions.
```

## Content Elements
1. **Document identification**: State this is a reply memorandum
2. **Party identification**: Identify movant
3. **Motion reference**: Reference the motion being supported

## Example

> Plaintiff EarthLink respectfully submits this reply memorandum of law in further support of its motion for spoliation sanctions against Defendant Charter Communications.
