# Preamble Instructions (Spoliation Sanctions Opposition)

## Purpose
The preamble identifies this document as an opposition to the spoliation motion.

## Format

```
MEMORANDUM OF LAW IN OPPOSITION TO
MOTION FOR SPOLIATION SANCTIONS

Defendant [NAME] respectfully submits this memorandum of law in opposition to Plaintiff's motion for spoliation sanctions.
```

## Content Elements
1. **Document identification**: State this is an opposition memorandum
2. **Party identification**: Identify responding party
3. **Motion reference**: Identify what motion is being opposed

## Examples

**Standard:**
> Defendant Charter Communications respectfully submits this memorandum of law in opposition to Plaintiff EarthLink's motion for spoliation sanctions.

**With reference to OSC:**
> Defendant respectfully submits this memorandum of law in opposition to Plaintiff's Order to Show Cause for spoliation sanctions, returnable [DATE].
