## XIV. IMPORTANT NOTES

1. **Timing Requirements:** Each motion type has specific timing requirements under the CPLR. Practitioners should consult the relevant statutory provisions before filing.

2. **Local Court Rules:** In addition to the CPLR, motions must comply with the Uniform Rules for Trial Courts (22 NYCRR) and any applicable Individual Part Rules of the assigned justice.

3. **Commercial Division:** Cases in the Commercial Division are subject to additional rules (22 NYCRR 202.70) that may affect motion practice.

4. **E-Filing:** Most New York State courts require electronic filing through NYSCEF. Motion papers must comply with e-filing requirements.

5. **Specialized Courts:** This memorandum addresses civil practice in Supreme Court and lower civil courts. Criminal Court, Family Court, Surrogate's Court, and other specialized courts have additional motion types specific to their subject matter jurisdiction.
