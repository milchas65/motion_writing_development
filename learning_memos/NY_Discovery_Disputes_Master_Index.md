## QUICK REFERENCE: COMMON ISSUES

### Before Filing a Discovery Motion
1. ✓ Confer in good faith (22 NYCRR 202.7, 202.20-f)
2. ✓ Document conferral efforts
3. ✓ Include good faith affirmation in motion
4. ✓ Consider motion to compel before sanctions

### Privilege Log Requirements
- **CPLR 3122(b):** Document-by-document (default)
- **Rule 11-b:** Categorical permitted if agreed or ordered
- **Must include:** Document type, subject matter, date, privilege claimed

### Deposition Limits
- **Number:** 10 per side
- **Duration:** 7 hours per witness
- **Extensions:** Good cause required

### Interrogatory Limits (Commercial Division)
- **Number:** 25 (including subparts)
- **Scope:** Witnesses, damages, documents (unless good cause)
- **Contention:** 30 days before discovery cut-off

### ESI Production
- **Format:** Native preferred; screenshots insufficient
- **Costs:** Producing party bears costs (unless shifted)
- **Holds:** Must be issued promptly; include text messages

### Sanctions Standards
- **Willful/Contumacious:** Inferred from repeated non-compliance
- **Spoliation:** Ordinary negligence suffices
- **Preference:** Lesser sanctions before striking pleadings
