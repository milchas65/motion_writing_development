# Preamble Instructions (OSC Application - Moving/Support)

## Format

```
MEMORANDUM OF LAW IN SUPPORT OF
ORDER TO SHOW CAUSE FOR [PRELIMINARY INJUNCTION / TRO / SPECIFIC RELIEF]

[Party] respectfully submits this memorandum of law in support of its application for an Order to Show Cause seeking [specific relief].
```

## Examples

### Preliminary Injunction
> Plaintiff respectfully submits this memorandum of law in support of its application for an Order to Show Cause seeking a preliminary injunction enjoining Defendant from [specific conduct].

### TRO
> Plaintiff respectfully submits this memorandum of law in support of its application for a temporary restraining order and preliminary injunction.

### Business Injunction
> Plaintiff respectfully submits this memorandum of law in support of its application for emergency injunctive relief pursuant to CPLR 6301 et seq.
