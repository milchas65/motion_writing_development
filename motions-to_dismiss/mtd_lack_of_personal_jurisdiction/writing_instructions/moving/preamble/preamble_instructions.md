# Preamble Instructions (Motion to Dismiss - Lack of Personal Jurisdiction)

## Format

```
MEMORANDUM OF LAW IN SUPPORT OF
DEFENDANT'S MOTION TO DISMISS FOR LACK OF PERSONAL JURISDICTION
PURSUANT TO CPLR 3211(a)(8)

Defendant respectfully submits this memorandum of law in support of its motion to dismiss for lack of personal jurisdiction.
```

## Federal Court
```
MEMORANDUM OF LAW IN SUPPORT OF
DEFENDANT'S MOTION TO DISMISS PURSUANT TO FED. R. CIV. P. 12(b)(2)

Defendant respectfully submits this memorandum of law in support of its motion to dismiss for lack of personal jurisdiction.
```
