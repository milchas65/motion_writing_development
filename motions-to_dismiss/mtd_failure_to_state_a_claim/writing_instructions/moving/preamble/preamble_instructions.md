# Preamble Instructions (Motion to Dismiss - Failure to State a Claim)

## Format

```
MEMORANDUM OF LAW IN SUPPORT OF
DEFENDANT'S MOTION TO DISMISS PURSUANT TO CPLR 3211(a)(7)

Defendant respectfully submits this memorandum of law in support of its motion to dismiss the complaint pursuant to CPLR 3211(a)(7) (failure to state a cause of action).
```

## Federal Court
```
MEMORANDUM OF LAW IN SUPPORT OF
DEFENDANT'S MOTION TO DISMISS PURSUANT TO FED. R. CIV. P. 12(b)(6)

Defendant respectfully submits this memorandum of law in support of its motion to dismiss for failure to state a claim upon which relief can be granted.
```
