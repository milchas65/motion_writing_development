# Preamble Instructions (Motion to Dismiss - Documentary Evidence)

## Format

```
MEMORANDUM OF LAW IN SUPPORT OF
DEFENDANT'S MOTION TO DISMISS PURSUANT TO CPLR 3211(a)(1)

Defendant respectfully submits this memorandum of law in support of its motion to dismiss the complaint pursuant to CPLR 3211(a)(1) (defense based upon documentary evidence).
```

## Note
CPLR 3211(a)(1) motions are based on documentary evidence that conclusively establishes a defense to the claim. The preamble should identify this specific ground.
